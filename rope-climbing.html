ROPE CLIMBING

Our rope climbing activities are the best in Britain, with an approval from the Outdoor Institiute of Climbing. They only accept the best, so you know that's what you'll be climbing.

BEGINNER CLIMBS

For those who just want to start their journey into rope cimbing, don't worry - we have all the courses you could want. From climbing 10-metre nets to scaling 20-metre high ropes, you'll learn and get better.

£30 for a 2 hour session

INTERMEDIATE CLIMBS

For those who want to challenge themselves and get better, these are for you. From courses that twist, to courses that go to the top of trees, you'll face nature and rise.

£40 for a 2 hour session

EXPERT CLIMBS

If you want a real challenge against nature, look no further. Ropes that go upside down, to very narrow ropes, to slippery ropes, wood plank ladders and rope swings: Train yourself to the extreme.

£90 for a 2-hour session
